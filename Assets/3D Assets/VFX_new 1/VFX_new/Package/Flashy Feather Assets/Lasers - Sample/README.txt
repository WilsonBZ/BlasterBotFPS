🔥 What’s Inside

This Unitypackage contains:

    🔵 Water Laser VFX

    🔴 Fire Laser VFX

    🧪 Demo Scene – A simple setup to preview both effects

    🎮 Ready-to-use in Unity URP (Universal Render Pipeline)

🛠️ Requirements

    Unity 2021.3+ (or newer)

    URP set up in your project

📦 How to Install

    Open your Unity URP project

    Drag and drop the .unitypackage into your Assets folder

    Open the demo scene at:
    Flashy Feather Assets/Lasers - Sample/Scene/Overview  -  Laser Sample.unity

    Test and drag the VFX in the "Prefabs" folder into your own scene
    
    Prefabs have a script. You can set the time duration and length multiplier of the lasers
    (changes Will be visible at runtime)
